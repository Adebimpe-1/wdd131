Our Mission

The Jesulana Hope Alive Foundation exists to bring hope and lasting change through community-driven programs centered on health care, education, and empowerment.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Jesulana began with a small group of dedicated volunteers committed to making a difference. Over the years, we have grown into a global foundation impacting thousands.

Our Team

We consist of healthcare professionals, educators, community organizers, and volunteers working hand-in-hand toward our shared vision.
